Summative Reflection

    My academic journey thus far has consisted of a combination of university courses and LinkedIn Learning courses. These courses have largely consisted of mathematics, sciences, literature, and computer science courses. The most prominent of the courses are Calculus I & II, Linear Algebra, and Data Structures & Algorithms. 


    So far, my participation in the co-op program hasn't had a large impact on my journey as a computer science student. Needing to take 250+ hours of virtual learning was tedious, however it did provide a good set of information. I haven't yet gained much from the co-op program, however I feel that going forward I will have better odds at getting and maintaining professional positions that will help me develop.


    If I were to start over again, the one thing I would focus on is creating a discrete goal from the start. . Lacking a good goal to work towards makes it much harder to figure out where to direct effort as it is difficult to tell what is and isn't helping to improve.


    That being said, the main goal I plan to focus on going forward regarding the co-op program is to get myself into a position. I believe that once I get my first co-op and complete it, that will give me a much better perspective on how to succeed in this discipline.
